Spanish Steps - Piazza di Spagna - Rome

The 'Spanish steps' in Rome, built in Rococo style between 1723 and 1726, are the steps that lead from the Piazza di Spagna square to the French monastery church Trinita dei Monti (built between 1502-1587). There are 135 steps and three different terraces, referring to the Holy Trinity (the Trinit√†). The top of the stairs near the Egyptian obelisk offers a beautiful view.
